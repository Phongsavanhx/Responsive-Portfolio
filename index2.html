About Me!

Hello! A little about me. Oh geez, I haven't done one of these since myspace. So I'm from the usually hot and sunny city of Tucson, Arizona. Born and raised. I've worked in restaurants since I could start working. Sushi kinda fell into my lap. One late night while bussing a table, the head sushi chef of Sakura asked me if I wanted to learn the trade. Enthusiastically I replied, "Of course!" And have been doing it ever since. That was about 10 years ago, and since then I've been doing it. Though, now, I feel I should move on to something else and try to increase what I know in life because the pursuit to better myself; mind, body, and soul is like a fire I can't put out. So I'm entering the exciting world of web development! I hope to use what I know to maaaaaaybe make menus for future up and coming restaurant owners and help them in their pursuit of online content to further everyone's love of sushi!
